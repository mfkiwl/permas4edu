Topology optimization of a prestressed beam

The entire design element is highlighted in shaded wire-frame mode, whereas the frozen region is displayed in blue color. The beam is pre-stressed by a screw and is connected to the foundation. A contact is defined between beam and foundation. The topology optimization can consider constraints from a static contact analysis and an eigenvalue analysis. For this purpose, the contacts are &quot;linearized&quot; by multipoint constraints (MPCs) based on the contact state.
